The University of Kansas Memorial Union
Come for the hot perks and stay for the people! Our 1000 Union employees are instrumental in making KU a special place for each student. Our efforts create campus community, opportunities for expression/exchange, experiential learning, student programs and essential services. The Union is a non-profit affiliate comprised of KU Dining, KU Bookstore, Student Programs and Events/Building Services. Each employee is essential in providing an excellent collegiate experience to every student.
