Benefits

The Union offers a competitive benefits package for our employees. As part of the Union family, you’ll have a package that covers your health, your well-being, your family and your future. We offer an exciting environment with internal advancement opportunity!

When choosing a new employer, it is important to consider all aspects. At the Union, we offer employees the opportunity to work with great people on exciting projects with a diversity and inclusion focus. As an employee you can collaborate with all departments that make up the Union- Dining Services, Bookstore, Building Services, and Student Programming.

If you want to work in a fast paced student focused environments, you’re at the right place!


Financial
  • Annual Salary Market Adjustment
    • Based on external and national data, your salary will increase based on applicable cost of living data.
  • Performance Awards
    • Based on company performance and bottom line results, a one time supplemental performance award is distributed. This percentage is based on your Annual Performance review. The higher your score, the higher the performance award.
  • Retirement Contribution
    • Employee starts contributing 5% pre tax to their retirement account; after one year, the company contributes 8.5%
  • Supplemental Accounts and Roth Retirement accounts available
Health
  • Blue Cross Blue Shield of Kansas health insurance
    • Three deductible options to choose from to fit your budget!
    • Company pays large portion of the monthly premiums
  • Health Savings Account with company contributes $500; employee can easily earn additional $500
  • Flex Spending, Dependent Care
  • Life and LTD coverage company paid
  • Additional purchases available for voluntary life insurance
Wellness
  • Local gym discounts at up to 50% off monthly rates
  • Wellness program with incentives- including free money!
  • Employee Assistance Program- financial, legal, mental health support- free and confidential
Work Life Balance
  • Sick and vacation time earned each month
  • Flexible schedule during school breaks
  • 10 paid holidays
  • Discretionary holiday day to be used during holiday time frame
  • Fantastic schedules for all departments- unbeatable in the dining industry
